Music takes me to a different place, a place filled with serenity, hope and no assignments. Music amplifies my emotions or changes it, depends.
I spend time playing my guitar whenever I can. I've given numerous performances as a solo musician as well as a part of a band- as the lead singer and guitarist.
I've passed the 5th grade of Trinity College London's examinination on plectrum guitar. I've also learnt Indian classical music for five years.
When I'm not playing music, I'm mostly listening to it. The genres I listen to vary greatly with my mood, I can go from Pink Floyd's 'Dark Side of the Moon' to Nakka Mukka and you won't see it coming.
